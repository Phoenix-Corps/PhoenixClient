16. Restricted Participation

 

Participation in this presale is strictly prohibited for individuals or entities located in, residing in, or associated with countries or regions that are subject to sanctions by the U.S. Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC). This includes, but is not limited to, countries currently on the OFAC Sanctions List. 

 

By participating, you confirm and warrant that: 

1. You are not located in, organized under the laws of, or a resident of an OFAC-sanctioned country. 

2. You are not identified as a Specially Designated National (SDN) or on any other U.S. or global sanctions list. 

3. You are not acting on behalf of any individual or entity that falls under the above restrictions. 

 

Failure to comply with these terms may result in immediate disqualification from the presale, and legal or regulatory action may follow where applicable. 

 

For more information on OFAC-sanctioned countries, please refer to the official [OFAC Sanctions List](https://home.treasury.gov/policy-issues/financial-sanctions/sanctions-programs-and-country-information).
